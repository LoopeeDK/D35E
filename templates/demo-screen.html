Foundry VTT - Player Orientation 3.5e SRD - Documentation

Your Hero: {{user.charname}}

You can find your hero in the sidebar by navigating to Actors list. To open character sheet, just click on the character name on the list and play around! You also get access to one zombie, so you can see how Monsters work!

Offline Character Sheet allows you to view your character sheet anywhere you are - and does not require Foundry server to be running! When your Foundry instance is up, you can make rolls from the Player's Companion - so no need to go to Foundry to use a weapon or roll that skill check!
